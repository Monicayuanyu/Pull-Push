First time pull-push

The plateau is hectic

with anemones, broken

mirror orchids, caper-quakes

beestung irises by the chipped

blue hive, the antioxidant horta.

Also a poem

The soote season, that bud and blome furth bringes

With grene hath clad the hill and eke the vale

The nightingale with fethers new she singes

The turtle to her make hath tolde her tale
